We ship what we can operate and sell what we run. The roadmap is deliberately phased so each
availability and multi-cloud claim is backed by production maturity before it's offered.

<Aside type="note">
Phases are sequenced by dependency, not fixed dates. Availability tiers become contractual only
after they're measured in production.
</Aside>

## Platform phases

**Phase 1 — Single-region managed Keycloak** <Badge text="Foundation" variant="success" />
- Fargate JVM data plane, serverless control plane, Aurora Multi-AZ, cells + placement.
- Automated backups and failover within a region. Target **99.9%**.

**Phase 2 — Analytics & AI config** <Badge text="Differentiators" variant="note" />
- Event Listener SPI → ClickHouse dashboards and audit exports ([Analytics](/docs/analytics/)).
- AI-config console: plain-English changes with dry-run diff ([Realms](/docs/realms/)).

**Phase 3 — Multi-region active-passive** <Badge text="Resiliency" variant="caution" />
- Aurora Global, DNS failover, tested promotion drills. Target **99.95%** once proven.

**Phase 4 — Multi-cloud (premium)** <Badge text="On demand" variant="default" />
- Cross-cloud warm standby (Aurora→Azure logical replication) for DR/sovereignty.
- YugabyteDB active-active for customers who need no failover gap → the **99.99%** tier.
- Offered when a customer contractually requires it, repriced as a premium — not the default.

## Feature tracks (in parallel)

| Track | Direction |
|---|---|
| **Identity depth** | Passkeys everywhere, richer adaptive auth, more IdP/federation connectors |
| **Migration tooling** | One-click importers for Auth0/Cognito/Ping, JIT federation helpers |
| **STS / token-broker** | Broader cloud targets, finer-grained scoping |
| **Compliance** | Formal SOC 2 attestation, then HIPAA — a multi-quarter program, reported honestly |
| **Operations** | More autonomous upgrades, capacity-aware autoscaling, per-cell economics tuning |

## What we deliberately are *not* claiming yet

<Aside type="caution">
- No blanket **99.99%** on day one — four-nines is earned on the active-active tier after
  production maturity.
- No **active-active across three clouds** as a default — it's a premium option, and the honest
  default is single-cloud multi-region active-passive.
- **Compliance** is described as audited vs in-progress, never as a blanket "compliant" badge.
</Aside>

Have a requirement that isn't here yet? It's probably a premium tier we can scope — talk to us.
