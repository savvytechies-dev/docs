## What this is

SavvyTechies runs **Keycloak** as a managed, multi-tenant platform: you get enterprise
identity (SSO, MFA, OIDC/SAML, RBAC) without operating Keycloak, Postgres, caching, upgrades,
or HA yourself. These docs describe **what we actually run** — a single-cloud, multi-region
architecture with optional cross-cloud standby — not an aspirational diagram.

## Principles

- **Sell what we run.** Availability is tiered by architecture (99.9% / 99.95% / 99.99%), backed after production maturity — not four-nines on day one.
- **No lock-in.** 100% open-source Keycloak core, standard APIs, exportable data.
- **Cost honesty.** ~60% cheaper than Auth0 at scale, from a low, mostly-fixed cost base.
